INDIAN ELEPHANT
 THE INDIAN ELEPHANT(ELEPHAS MAXIMUS INDICUS) IS ONE OF THREE RECOGNIZED OF THE ASIAN ELEPHANT AND NATIVE TO MAINLAND ASIA. SINCE 1986, ELEPHAS HAS BEEN LISTED AS ENDANGERED BY IUCN AS THE POPULATION HAS DECLINED BY AT LEAST 50% OVER THE THREE GENERATIONS, ESTIMATED TO BE 60-75 YEARS. ASIAN ELEPHANTS ARE THREATENED BY HABITATED LOSS, DEGRADATION AND FRGMENTATION.
