NATIONAL E-GOVERNANCE PLAN
THE NATIONAL E-GOVERNANCE PLANOF GOVERNMENT SEEKS TO LAY THE FOUNDATION AND PROVIDES THE IMPETUS FOR LONG-TERM GROWTH OF E-GOVERNANACE WITHIN THE COUNTRY. THE PLAN SEEKS TO CREATE THE RIGHT GOVERNANCE AND INSTITUTIONAL MECHANISMS, SET UP THE CORE INFRASTRUCTURE AND POLICIES AND IMPLEMEMT A NUMBER OF MISSION MODE PROJECTS AT THE CENTER STATE AND INTREGATED SERVICE LEVIS TO CREATE CITIZEN-CENTRIC AND BUSINESS-CENTRIC ENVIRONMENTFOR GOVERNANCE
