TRAVEL AND TOURISM

TRAVEL REFERS TO TAKINGA TRIP OR A JOURNEY TO ANOTHER PLACE, AND TOURISM REFERS TO THE ACT OF TRAVELLING AS WELL AS TO THE BUSNISS SIDE OF THE TRAVEL INDUSTRY. TRAVEL CAN ALSO BEUSED TO DESCRIBE THE ACT OF MOVING FROM ONE PLACE TO ANOTHER.

ADDITIONALLY,IT IS USED INBASKETBALL TO DESCRIBE WHAT HAPPEN IF SOMEONE TAKES MORE THAN TWO STEPS WITHOUT DRIBBLING THE BALL.
