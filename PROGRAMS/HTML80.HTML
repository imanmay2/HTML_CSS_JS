India : Our Country


India is spread over a large geographical area. In terms of area, it
is the 7th largest country while in terms of population, it is the 2nd
largest country in the world. Some facts about India are as follows:
  • Area : Approx. 3.28 million sq. km
  • North to South extension : Approx. 3,200 km
  • East to West extension : Approx. 2900 km
