WILDLIFE-D DEFINATION



WILDLIFE TRADITIONALLY REFERS TO NON-DOMESTICATED ANIMAL SPICES, BUT HAS COME TO INCLUDE ALL PLANTS, FUNGI, AND OTHER ORGANISMS THAT GROW OR LIVE WILD IN AREA WITHOUT BEING INTRODUCED BY HUMANS. IT CAN BE FOUND IN ALL ECO-SYSTEMS. DESERTS,FORESTS,RAIN-FORESTS,PLAINS,GRASSLANDS AND OTHER AREAS INCLUDING THE MOST DEVELOPED URBAN AREAS SITES,ALL HAVE DISTINCT FORMS OF WILDLIFE.
