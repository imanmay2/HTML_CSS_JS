NORTH AND SOUTH TOURISM







THE PREFFERRED DESTINATION INCLUDE- SHIMLA, KINNAUR, LAHAUL, AND THE DISTRICTS OF SPITI, CHAMBA, KANGRA, MANDI NAINTAL, KULLU , MANLI, MASSOURIE, DALHUSE, KURFI, KASHMIR VALLEY IN WINTERS WITH SNOWFALL BEING THE MAIN ATTRACTION. RAJASTHAN AS ALAWAYS, WILL REMAINA FAVOURITE AND PLACES LIKE UDALPUR, JAIPAR, JODHPUR, BIKANER ETC WILL SEE A MIX OF DOMESTIC AND FOREIGN TOURISTS.BESIDES, THE TOP DESTINATIONS IN SOUTH WOULD INCLUDE OOTY, MYSORE, PUDICHERRY, RAMESHWARAN, AND GOA, MAHABALESHWAR IN THE WEST, ACCORDING TO JUST CONCLUDED SURVEY CONDUCTED BY THE ASSOCIATED CHAMBERS OF THE COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY OF INDIA(ASSOCHAM).
